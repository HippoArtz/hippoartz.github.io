About

I paint women suspended in surreal landscapes, their bodies reshaped by violence and survival. My work sits in the tension between oppression and defiance, where figures are fractured, bound, or silenced but never erased. They bloom through rupture and stand unbowed—embodiments of pain transformed into power.
