About

Hippo.Artz paints figures suspended in surreal landscapes—bodies reshaped by violence, survival, and reclamation. The work sits in the tension between oppression and defiance, inviting viewers to look longer than comfort allows.

Meet the Acrylic Alchemist

Welcome to HIPPO.ARTZ: a novice apothecary of experimentation with color, texture, and storytelling. Hippo.Artz is a self-taught acrylic painter and visual storyweaver. The canvases explore how emotion and identity take shape through bold strokes, surreal dreamscapes, and Black Girl Magic.

“I am a pessimist if I am not careful.” — Octavia E. Butler

Every piece is layered with emotion, lived experience, and a shimmer of Black Girl Magic. Acrylic on canvas and unfinished wood is the primary medium.

Outside the studio: public health work and two cats, Fawn and Goatee. This site is both portfolio and playground—browse, reflect, connect. Be well.

Current Project Themes

  • Queens — Beauty, visibility, identity, representation, belonging.
  • Dreamscapes and Nightmares — Without darkness, how would we know the light? Eerie, surreal, close to the veil.
  • Unheard Echoes — Chains, crowns, tears—the weight and endurance of Black women.
  • In Memory of — Tender reckonings, grief-work, remembrance.
